why choose us?

ataffysa's bakery always makes improvements by building new factories and using more modern machinery and equipment facilities to produce quality, safe and halal products. and has also obtained a Halal Certificate from MUI, and a distribution permit from BPOM RI. With the recognition of the certification body, ataffisya's bakery will be more optimistic in facing the future. To face the growth of market demand in the future, ataffysa's bakery is committed to investing in technology and human resources.

Established on May 1, 2005, Ataffysa’s bakery started small-scale bakery and cake production. To meet the increasing demand for bakery & cake markets, Ataffysa’s bakery made improvements by building a new factory and using more modern machinery and equipment facilities to produce quality, safe and halal products. In addition, improving the quality of human resources is also carried out in order to keep up with the development of the company and answer customer needs. The hard work shown by the Management and all employees of Ataffysa’s bakery through the improvements and refinements that have been made has paid off by obtaining a Halal Certificate from MUI, a distribution permit from BPOM RI. With the recognition from the certification body, Ataffysa’s bakery is more optimistic in looking to the future. To face the growth of market demand in the future, Ataffysa’s bakery is committed to investing in technology and human resources.
